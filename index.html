About Me

Phong Truong,
Full Stack Developer

Hi! My name is Phong Truong. I graduated California State University, Fullerton with a degree in psychology because I love the way the human mind works and how we interact with each other. I have used what I learned to help improve sales, train employees, and increase productivity.

While I love psychology my true passion has always been technology. I have been tinkering and troubleshooting computers since I was 6 years old. In my free time I keep up with the latest tech news and trends. That being said, I never got into coding; until a former colleague told me what he has been up to and showed me all the cools apps he was making for fun. I looked up some beginner coding sites, and after few lessons I was hooked.

Shortly after, I enrolled at Learning Fuze, an accelerated Web Development Program. During my time there I learned different languages such as Javascript, CSS, HTML, PHP, and SQL. Even though every week was like a rollercoaster ride of information, I had a blast designing, strategizing, and coding amazing apps such as a dynamic JavaScript board game and a full-stack online store. During one of the hackathons, we even built a geolocation-based game with a custom API that sends and receives data from multiple endpoints such as Yelp and Google Maps.

I truly believe technology is key to bringing people together. I'm both excited and eager to deepen my knowledge and further improve my coding abilities to help people share experiences and connect with each other.
